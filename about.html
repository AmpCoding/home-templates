My Background and Development Philosophy

The Journey Begins

I started my professional journey with a deep interest in how digital experiences are constructed. My focus has always been on learning the foundational elements first, which is why I prioritize structured HTML and well-organized, semantic code. I believe that a website must be fundamentally sound before any visual layer is added.

Current Focus and Future Goals

Currently, I am dedicated to mastering CSS, specifically in creating responsive layouts and ensuring cross-browser compatibility. My goal is to eventually move into front-end frameworks and full-stack development. I am passionate about creating accessible and user-friendly web applications that serve a clear purpose.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy learning about emerging web technologies and contributing to online communities focused on coding best practices.
