Things That Inspire Me

Sustainable Design and Tech

I am deeply interested in how technology can be used to promote sustainability and ethical consumption. This extends to web development, where I focus on optimizing performance to reduce the carbon footprint of the applications I build. Efficient code is inherently more sustainable.

From minimizing network requests to employing modern image formats, every detail contributes to a faster, greener web. This interest informs my approach to project management and design decisions, always prioritizing lightweight, fast solutions.

Photography and Visual Arts

Beyond coding, I enjoy digital photography and creating visual art. I find that the principles of composition and negative space in art are directly applicable to good user interface design. If you'd like to see some of my work, please visit the Gallery Page!
