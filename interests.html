Things That Keep Me Engaged

Professional Passions

Beyond the required coursework, my main professional interests lie in the area of Data Visualization and User Experience (UX) Design. I find immense satisfaction in taking complex data sets and transforming them into interactive, visually appealing charts and graphs that tell a clear story.

  • Semantic HTML structure
  • Modern CSS (Flexbox and Grid)
  • Accessibility standards (WCAG)
  • JavaScript fundamentals

Personal Hobbies

When I step away from the keyboard, my interests shift to the analog world. I enjoy hiking and exploring national parks, which allows me to disconnect and recharge. I also have a growing interest in photography, primarily focusing on landscapes and urban decay.

I am constantly looking for ways to blend my personal hobbies with my professional skills, often leading to creative web projects inspired by nature or travel.
